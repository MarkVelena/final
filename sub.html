Sub-City Distributor: A sub-city distributor operates within a smaller area within a city or urban region. They are responsible for distributing beauty products to retailers, beauty salons, and other businesses within their designated sub-city area. Sub-city distributors work closely with manufacturers or brands to ensure the availability and quality of beauty products. They manage the logistics and transportation of products within their sub-city area and provide support to retailers to enhance product sales and visibility.
